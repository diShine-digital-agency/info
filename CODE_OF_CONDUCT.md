# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This is a professional open-source space. We expect everyone who participates — through issues, pull requests, or discussions — to:

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person
- Accept different perspectives and approaches
- Help keep the conversation useful for everyone

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and anything that makes this space hostile or unwelcoming.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all activity within our GitHub organization — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other interactions tied to our repositories.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates these standards, contact us at [dishine.it](https://dishine.it/). We take these reports seriously and will respond promptly.

Violations may result in comments being removed, contributions being rejected, or access being revoked — depending on severity.
